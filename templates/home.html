CoronaCast

A simple yet interpretable system for predicting daily COVID cases and deaths for US states.




Most people have no idea how a prediction is being made. They just blindly enter in numbers and letters, click a button, and wait for a result. Hence, they are less likely to trust the result they get. With COVID-19, knowing daily cases and deaths is extremely important and trust needs to be inherent in a prediction. That's why CoronaCast was built. Using easy-to-understand text-based and graphical explanations, CoronaCast aims to explain the number of predicted COVID-19 cases and deaths for any given US state. Doing so will build user trust in the system, and that is what making predictions is all about.
